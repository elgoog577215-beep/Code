## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: 复杂评测数据必须具备语义多样性

系统 SHALL ensure autogenerated complex fixtures are not merely repeated variants of a small number of templates.

#### Scenario: semantic template coverage is checked

- **WHEN** complex fixtures are loaded
- **THEN** they SHALL include at least 14 generator template slugs
- **AND** they SHALL include at least 12 distinct bug patterns
- **AND** generated live candidates SHALL cover at least 12 distinct bug patterns

#### Scenario: fine-grained root cause coverage is checked

- **WHEN** complex fixtures are loaded
- **THEN** primary root causes SHALL cover at least 12 distinct fine-grained diagnosis tags
- **AND** each primary fine-grained tag SHALL exist in the diagnosis taxonomy

### Requirement: 复杂样本生成器必须防止同质重复回退

系统 SHALL include quality gates that fail when generated fixture diversity collapses.

#### Scenario: semantic source groups collapse

- **WHEN** generated source files are normalized by removing variant-only helper names
- **THEN** the fixture tests SHALL fail if fewer than 14 semantic source groups remain
